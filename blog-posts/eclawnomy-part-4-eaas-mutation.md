## The Managed Gap

A powerful tool is useless if you don't have the time to maintain it. Managing a fleet of serverless agents, securing their AWS accounts, and ensuring they are always running the latest version of their "intelligence" is a full-time job.

Most businesses don't want to be "AI Operators." They want **Results.**

## ClawMore: The Evolutionary Hub

[ClawMore](https://clawmore.ai/) is the managed service that bridges the gap between Open Source and Enterprise Reliability. It operates on a **Hub-and-Spoke** architecture, specifically designed to lower the knowledge barrier we discussed in Part 1.

### 1. Account Vending & Isolation

Every ClawMore client gets their own, dedicated AWS account. We don't share infrastructure. We don't share data. Our "Vending Machine" programmatically creates an isolated sandbox where your agents can work without risk of cross-tenant leakage.

### 2. The Mutation Tax ($1.00 per Win)

We don't believe in "Seat-based" pricing. In the **Eclawnomy**, we use the **Mutation Tax.**

- **Platform Fee**: $29/mo (Covers managed infra and basic compute).
- **Mutation Fee**: $1.00 per successful agentic change (a "Mutation").

If your agents don't improve your codebase, you don't pay. Our incentives are perfectly aligned with your success.

### 3. Co-evolution & The Harvester

By allowing our "Harvester" agent to extract anonymous **Innovation Patterns** from your specific optimizations, you contribute to the collective intelligence of the Hub. In exchange, we waive the Mutation Tax.

**Global intelligence scales at the speed of the fastest innovator.**

For $29/mo, an SMB doesn't just get a tool. They get a managed, evolving, and self-improving node in a global intelligence network.

The "IT Department" is no longer a cost center. It’s a **subscription to continuous evolution.**
