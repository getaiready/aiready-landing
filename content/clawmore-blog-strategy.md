# ClawMore Blog Strategy: "The Mutation Log"

This document outlines the content strategy for the **ClawMore** platform blog, focusing on managed infrastructure, security, and the business of evolution.

## 🎯 Core Themes

1.  **Managed Reliability**: Why businesses shouldn't be their own AI DevOps.
2.  **Safety & Isolation**: Zero-trust architecture for agents.
3.  **Network Intelligence**: The value of the collective co-evolution model.

---

## 📝 Blog Outlines

### 1. Why $0 Idle is the Only Way to Scale (The Economics of Zero)

- **Hook**: Most AI platforms charge for "access." We charge for "improvement."
- **Key Points**:
  - The "Fax Machine" Problem: Why traditional VMs and Docker containers are too slow and expensive for high-frequency agentic tasks.
  - How ServerlessClaw allows for 10,000 agents to exist in wait-state for $0.
  - Predictable billing: Scaling from 1 to 1,000,000 tasks without linear cost growth.

### 2. Safety First: Isolating Intelligence with SST and AWS Vending

- **Hook**: "I love the agent, but I don't trust the internet."
- **Key Points**:
  - **Account Vending**: Every client gets an isolated sandbox. No cross-tenant data leaks.
  - **Principle of Least Privilege**: How we restrict agents to only the files or tools they need for the specific mutation.
  - **The Human-in-the-Loop Override**: Trusting your agents with a kill-switch.

### 3. The Harvester: How Your Wins Help Everyone (And Lower Your Bill)

- **Hook**: Collective intelligence is the ultimate moat.
- **Key Points**:
  - Explaining the **Co-evolution Protocol**.
  - **The Co-evolution Dividend**: Trade anonymous innovation patterns for lower platform fees.
  - Growing together: When one 'Recruitment Claw' gets smarter, they all do (while keeping your private data private).
